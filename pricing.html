Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 30-day free trial work?

Sign up and get instant access to all Starter plan features for 30 days. No credit card required. After the trial ends, you can upgrade to a paid plan or your account will be suspended.

What happens when my trial expires?

Your account will be suspended until you purchase a subscription. Your data remains safe and will be restored when you upgrade.

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans if you're not satisfied with our service.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees, no hidden costs. You only pay for your chosen plan at the displayed price.

How secure is my code?

We use enterprise-grade encryption and never store your original source code. All obfuscation happens in secure, isolated environments.
